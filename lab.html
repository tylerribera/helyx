Radical Transparency

Inside the Helyx Lab

We believe you deserve to know exactly how your research compounds are made, tested, and shipped. Here's our entire process — no black boxes, no vague claims.

Our Standards

Quality at Every Step

SPPS Synthesis

All peptides are manufactured using solid-phase peptide synthesis (SPPS) under controlled conditions, ensuring precise amino acid sequences and minimal impurities.

HPLC Purification

Crude peptides are purified via high-performance liquid chromatography to achieve ≥99% purity. We accept nothing less than research-grade standards.

Mass Spectrometry ID

Every batch undergoes mass spectrometry analysis to confirm molecular identity. The measured mass must match the theoretical mass within acceptable tolerance.

Third-Party COAs

We never self-certify. Every Certificate of Analysis comes from independent, accredited third-party laboratories. Full, unredacted reports are available upon request.

Climate-Controlled Storage

All inventory is stored at -20°C in dedicated cold storage units. Temperature logs are maintained and auditable to guarantee compound stability from synthesis to shipment.

Insulated Shipping

Orders ship in insulated packaging with cold packs when necessary. Every shipment is tracked, and peptides are sealed in inert-gas-flushed vials to prevent degradation.

From Synthesis to Shipment

Our Process, Step by Step

Every Helyx peptide follows this exact workflow. No shortcuts, no exceptions.

Step 01

Raw Material Sourcing

Amino acids and reagents are sourced from vetted, GMP-compliant suppliers. Each incoming material is inspected and documented before entering the synthesis pipeline.

Step 02

Solid-Phase Synthesis

Peptides are assembled using automated SPPS systems. The process is monitored at every coupling cycle to ensure correct amino acid incorporation and sequence fidelity.

Step 03

Cleavage & Crude Analysis

The peptide is cleaved from the resin and analyzed in crude form via analytical HPLC to assess initial purity and identify any deletion sequences or side products.

Step 04

HPLC Purification

Preparative HPLC purification isolates the target peptide from impurities. Fractions are collected and re-analyzed to confirm ≥99% purity before proceeding.

Step 05

Lyophilization

Purified peptide solutions are freeze-dried (lyophilized) to produce a stable, dry powder. This maximizes shelf life and ensures consistent reconstitution properties.

Step 06

Third-Party Testing

Samples from every batch are sent to independent, accredited labs for HPLC purity analysis and mass spectrometry identity confirmation. No batch ships without passing both tests.

Step 07

Packaging & Cold Shipment

Verified peptides are sealed in sterile, inert-gas-flushed vials, stored at -20°C, and shipped with insulated packaging and cold packs to maintain chain-of-cold integrity.
